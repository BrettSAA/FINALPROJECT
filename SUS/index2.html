How it works
Step 1: Create your listing

It’s free and easy to create a listing on SellUrSurplus. Describe your surplus equipment or parts, how much you would like to sell it for, and add photos and details. Our experiences industrial equipment professionals can recommend competitive pricing, but what you charge is always up to you.

Step 2: Make a deal

If we really like your equipment, we might contact you directly and buy it from you right away. In other cases we might have a buyer ready and waiting for you in which case we will contact you to take your equipment on consignment. Lastly, if its not right for us to buy from you right now, it will be displayed on our platform for free to millions of industry professionals from around the world.

Step 3: Get paid

SellUrSurplus's secure payment system means you never have to deal with money directly. Our buyers make a down payment for the initial purchase that is not charged until you accept the request. A second payment is collected within 48 hours, and you are paid automatically once the equipment has been shipped minus a 3% service fee. You can be paid via PayPal, direct deposit, ACH, international money wire, among other ways.
