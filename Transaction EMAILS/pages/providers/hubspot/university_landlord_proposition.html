How we can work together.

List SAA as your trusted accommodation resource with a link through to our site. They can book on our site as well as benefit from exclusive discounts. Another option is to add an SAA landing page to the housing area of your site. Students fill out a short form and one of our booking agents will reach out to your students with housing recommendations within 1 working day.

You can link through to SAA in admissions emails to students, or indeed share a link to our landing page. Our inventory caters for a wide range of budgets; from simple, individual rooms in shared apartments to luxurious penthouses suitable for large groups.

We are able to attend welcome events, introduce our brand to your students and help them find accommodation for their stay. Please do give us as much notice as possible if you'd like us to prepare anything.

We can deliver marketing materials like posters, leaflets, and cards for you to keep in your international office. It can be helpful to distribute these materials to students looking for accommodation for their outbound studies too!
